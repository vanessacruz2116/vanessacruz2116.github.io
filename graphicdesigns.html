Photo Effect

Photo effects are predefined algorithms that enable you to add special effects to your images.

Photo Manipulation

The art of altering an image to express what you want, rather than what the original image may have displayed. It is done for deceitfulness and artistic reasons.


Vector

Vector art is a form of digital illustration and its use is increasing every day. Vector graphics is the use of geometrical primitives such as points, lines, curves, and shapes or polygons.


Photo Restoration

Artistic process used to fix old photos or restore damaged photographs.
